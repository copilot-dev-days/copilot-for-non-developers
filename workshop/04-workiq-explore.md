# Part 4: Exploring Your Work with WorkIQ

> **Time:** ~5 minutes  
> **Goal:** Get comfortable asking Copilot about your real M365 data — before the structured demos, just explore

---

## Just Start Asking

Now that WorkIQ is connected to your Microsoft 365, Copilot has access to your email, calendar, documents, Teams channels, people directory, and Planner. You don't need to learn any special syntax — just ask questions the same way you'd ask a colleague.

This section is unstructured on purpose. Pick the prompts that feel most relevant to your work.

---

## 📅 Calendar & Time

```
what meetings do I have today and tomorrow?
```

```
how many hours did I spend in meetings last week?
```

```
do I have any back-to-back meetings this week with no breaks?
```

```
what's my first available 30-minute slot this Friday afternoon?
```

```
which recurring meetings have I attended the most this month?
```

---

## 📧 Email & Messages

```
what emails are waiting for my reply?
```

```
show me everything from [person's name] in the last two weeks
```

```
find any email threads where I was CC'd but never responded
```

```
what are the most common topics in my inbox this week?
```

```
show me unread emails flagged as important
```

---

## 👤 People & Org

```
who is [person's name] and what team are they on?
```

```
show me the org chart for [manager's name]
```

```
who are the direct reports of [name]?
```

```
who at the company works on [project or product name]?
```

---

## 📄 Documents & Files

```
find my most recently edited documents
```

```
show me any files that were shared with me this week that I haven't opened
```

```
find PowerPoint presentations I worked on in the last month
```

```
search my OneDrive for anything related to "budget"
```

---

## ✅ Planner & Tasks

```
what Planner tasks are assigned to me this week?
```

```
show me all my overdue tasks across all plans
```

```
search all my Planner tasks for "review"
```

```
which tasks have no due date set?
```

---

## 💬 Teams Channels

```
summarize what happened in the [channel name] channel this week
```

```
show me any Teams messages where I was directly mentioned today
```

```
what decisions were made in the [team name] channel this week?
```

```
find any open questions or action items from my Teams chats today
```

---

## Prompt Reference

| Category | Example Prompt |
|----------|---------------|
| Calendar | `how many hours did I spend in meetings last week?` |
| Email | `what emails are waiting for my reply?` |
| People | `who is [name] and what team are they on?` |
| Documents | `find my most recently edited documents` |
| Tasks | `what Planner tasks are assigned to me this week?` |
| Teams | `summarize what happened in the [channel] channel this week` |
